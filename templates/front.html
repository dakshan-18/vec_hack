What is Mental Health Care Assistant?

Mental health refers to a person's emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Good mental health helps individuals cope with the demands of life, work productively, and contribute to their communities.
